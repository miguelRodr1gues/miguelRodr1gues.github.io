Melbourne Mornings

In the heart of Melbourne, where the Yarra flows,
A city awakens, as the sunrise glows.
Trams hum softly down Collins Street,
Mixing past and future, where time seems to meet.

The laneways whisper, with tales untold,
Art on the walls, vibrant and bold.
Coffee brews strong in hidden cafes,
Fuel for the souls that wander these days.

A symphony of cultures, voices entwined,
Stories of journeys, hopes undefined.
From the Botanic Gardens to St Kilda's shore,
Melbourne embraces, offering more.

Beneath the skyscrapers and open skies,
A city of dreams, where freedom flies.
Melbourne, you dance to your own gentle tune,
A place of warmth, under the Southern moon.

Miguel Rodrigues | 2º Engenharia Informática | Lusófona | DIW 2024
